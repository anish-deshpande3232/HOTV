⚡ Process

How It Works

Four simple steps from farm to consumer — every action immutably recorded on the blockchain.

🌱
Farmer Creates Batch
Producer records harvest details, uploads photos and certifications. Metadata pinned to IPFS, batch ID minted on-chain.
🏢
Company Processes
Company accepts custody, verifies batch details and sends to certified lab for quality testing. All custody transfers recorded.
🧪
Lab Tests & Signs
Certified lab performs tests, uploads signed report to IPFS and submits results on-chain. Digital signature ensures authenticity.
📱
Consumer Verifies
Consumer scans QR code or enters batch ID to instantly verify origin, lab results, certifications and full supply chain history.
